About us

We crawl all night long

Arguably the best Pub Crawl ever. Crazy challenges and awesome guides that are always prepared to spice up your night out in Zagreb. The meeting point is the main square next to the statue at 8:45pm every day in a week.

The bar crawl includes:
  • Beer for beer pong
  • 4 shots of rakija (1 rakija as a welcome drink at each location)
  • Entrance to the club
  • Awesome local guides
  • Crazy challenges with rewards
